The Conceptional Understanding of becoming a web developer, is that you'll signup with a bootcamp 10-12 weeks later you'll be getting courted by all the Tech Giants. Such as Google, Microsoft, Activision, or many others of the fortune 500 companies.The fact of it all is that all of those companies need or want you, but they will not come to you unless you have displayed skills on a very advanced level.I would love for you all to stay positive and keep sharpening those skills personal, professional, and people skills! Stay reading, researching, and renewing your mindset and skillset. Make sure you join a program truly tailored to help you rise above the plateau think of your development process as an athlete. If your core team isn't pushing and ensuring you along the process then your performance will show as such. Now, if your core team is engaging, uplifting, and brutally honest you will leave nothing to be imagined. Top-notch performances will be a routine. You must have a developer's environment to do so :-).

My Conclusion

My closing statement is to remind you that you are a strategist, and no strategy is completely perfect. We strategize layouts for both the digital and physical realms, we are the doers and thinkers, or the thinkers and doers. We have one obligation to ourselves, that is to enjoy life, set a daily routine, be courteous, and be willing to work well alone and with a team. Get ready to set the tone for the future cause we are the new world shapers. Let's make the world a better place for the future.
